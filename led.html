Illuminated Signs

Illuminated signs are a visually appealing option when a business or person wants to attract people to your business. They’re ideal to grab the eye of passer-byes, pedestrians or to increase noticeability, especially at night. LED signsmakes this possible with their bright displays as they increase visibility. They are visible from a distance when the profile might be low.
There is nothing more important than to be known to a business. Without the right visibility, your establishment is just like every other lost on the roadside, a well-placed LED signs can help with that.
We custom make the LED sign letters, Edge lit letters, Trim cap letters, Back lit letters, SS Trim letters , flex glow boards, Pole signs, Reception / Lobby signs, LED scrolling display, ACP routed signs, Dot LED letters, for your business with the ability to attract attention in any weather and at any time of day. This is a major marketing win!

LED sign boards :

Edge lit letters :

Edge Lit letters can make quite the impression, whether used in interior or exterior settings. Edge Lit letters are designed to outshine With over 15 different design, color and variations, these striking illuminated letters are the perfect choice for dramatic retail or corporate signage.

Trim cap letters :

Trim cap channel letter is designed with trim cap or border of the regular channel letter, it has face around the acrylic letters.

Back lit Letters :

Back lit letters also known as Halo lit letters – Used when a more refined look is important. Halo lit letters are typically installed with spacers to maintain a gap between the rear of the letters and the wall or other mounting surface in order to allow the light to exit through the rear of the letters and reflect off the wall, creating what is known as the halo effect.

Flex glow boards :

Glow signs board is a special type of sign board. It illuminates at night. It is made of combination of Neon,acrylic, PVC flex, polycarbonate and others. It is A printed vinyl/flex or some other sheet fixed on a metal box frame in which lights(tube lights,bulbs or cf ls) are fixed to make bill/sign glow at night or in dark enviorments.these are used in quick turning areas, accident prone areas, dark zones, lane merging zones and centre verge because at night when light focused on these boards they absorb some light & bright for some time with the help of absorbed light.

LED scrolling display :

Even in today’s ever changing industry, classic single, multi-line and matrix indoor LED scrolling Displays remain a simple, cost effective way of achieving a high impact display. LED scrolling Displays can be used as a stand alone product or incorporated into a much larger display that can be used to present highly visible changing messages to customers, employees and guests throughout your organisation.

ACP routed signs :

Reverse cut with Embossed acrylic letters For an added dimensional effect, the letters and logo can be be cut out of the face and then raised acrylic letters push through the face. It’s completed with a translucent acrylic backer in your colors. Low voltage LEDs assembled inside light up the push thru acrylic letters. The maximum size for a single piece is 3.5’ x 11’.
