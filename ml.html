Non-Lit Signs

Non-illuminated signs & signage are a very cost-effective way of advertising your business, particularly in well lit areas, and can be made from a wide variety of materials and colors and are ideal for shopping centers, industrial units, hotels, shops & restaurants. We offer a wide range of options like Metal letters, Engraving signs, Reception / Lobby signs, Acrylic wall mounting and Foam letters for your business with the ability to attract attention in any weather and at any time of day and night.

Metal letters :

Our indoor and outdoor cut metal letters are available in wide range of metals including aluminum, stainless steel, Copper, Brass and Bronze and come with a lifetime guarantee, The water jet letters are cut from solid plate metal using an advanced, precision cutting technique. They range in thickness from 3mm to 25mm and in height from as small as 25mm up to 1200mm.
If you are looking for letters with greater depth or thickness, consider our Fabricated stainless steel letters, aluminum letters, Backlit letters.

Engraving signs :

Create custom engraved signs for any purpose, choosing from a wide variety of attractive and durable materials, Stainless Steel, Brass, Titanium Gold acrylic. Easy-to-use. Design tools help you get exactly the look you want.

Our corporate lobby signs are available in a wide variety of materials, finishes, colors and mounting options, so you’ll end up with the sign that is ideal for your space, image, decor and budget.

Foam letters :

Foam letters are ideal for a wide variety of interior signage needs including retail displays, special event signage, retail POP and tradeshow displays. All of our foam letters, are made from Gatorfoam board, which is a structural foam sandwiched between a hard facing exterior to provide extra durability and a smooth surface.
Our foam letters can be used unpainted in natural white or black or painted in a standard or custom color with a matte or semi-gloss finish.They can also be laminated with a real metal laminate in brass, aluminum and bronze finishes. Foam letters are typically not the best solution for long term or permanent signage. For longer durability, you can go with acrylic letters.
